WELCOME TO DANNHAUSER MALT

DANNHAUSER MALT(PTY)LTD. is a malting company based in a small previously mining town of Dannhauser in Nothern Kwazulu-Natal. We are the producer of the MALT and brand leader of AMANDHLA MALT.

We producer two main Products: Grain Sorghum malt and Maize Malt, which is packaged in different weight classes from 1Kg to 60 Kg

We manage our entire supply chain from sourcing and transportation, storage , warehousing and packaging. We are about brand building, product quality and customer care, while leading the way for socially conscious businesses

Grain sorghum Malt is the main ingredient in the traditional beer making process. Maize Malt is also used in the traditional beer making process but with a less malty taste
